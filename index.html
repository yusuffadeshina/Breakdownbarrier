Example: At Break Down Barriers, we started with a simple belief: every young person, no matter their background, deserves the chance to succeed. In Bristol, too many young people face challenges that stop them from staying in education and reaching their potential.

That's why we exist — to break down those barriers. Through personalised mentoring programmes, we help young people overcome obstacles, grow their confidence, and build a brighter future.

This isn't about what we do; it's about who we do it for. Every decision, programme, and connection we make is focused on supporting the young people we serve. Together, we're building a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our mission:

Break Down Barriers is dedicated to keeping young people in Bristol engaged through personalised mentoring programmes. We believe in empowering every young person, regardless of their background or circumstances, to unlock their fullest potential. Our mission is to inspire, support, and transform lives by breaking down barriers and creating opportunities for growth and success

Our values:

Our values are the foundation of everything we do, guiding our decisions and driving our mission forward:
Empowerment – We believe in equipping young people with the tools, confidence, and opportunities to thrive.
Quality – We are committed to delivering excellence in every programme, partnership, and interaction. Together, these core values shape our approach, ensuring we set the highest standards in empowering young people and breaking down barriers to their success
